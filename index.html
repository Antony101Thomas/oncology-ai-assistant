Capabilities

Everything evidence-based,
nothing invented.

Every claim is traced back to a trusted source. If evidence isn't found, the assistant refuses to answer rather than guess.

📚
Local PDF Retrieval
Indexes NCCN, uploaded guidelines, and handbooks. Hybrid BM25 + vector search ensures no relevant passage is missed.
🔬
PubMed Live Search
Queries PubMed in real time for recent research articles and abstracts directly relevant to your oncology question.
🧪
ClinicalTrials.gov
Finds active and recent clinical trials matching the condition or drug in your question — with status and NCT IDs.
🛡️
Validator & Confidence
Every answer is checked: numeric facts verified against sources, term overlap measured, and a confidence score assigned.
🚦
Smart Query Router
Classifies every question as conceptual, recency-seeking, or precision lookup — and picks the right retrieval strategy automatically.
⛔
Principled Refusals
When evidence is insufficient or the question is off-topic, the system refuses cleanly rather than generating unreliable content.
