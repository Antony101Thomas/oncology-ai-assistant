About ONCO AI

ONCO AI is an evidence-based oncology assistant powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).


Every answer is grounded in trusted sources:

  • NCCN Guidelines — Indexed patient PDFs for 20+ cancer types
  • PubMed — 36M+ peer-reviewed research articles (live API)
  • ClinicalTrials.gov — 500K+ registered trials (live API)

You can upload your own oncology PDFs using the paperclip icon.


⚠ For clinical reference only. Not a substitute for professional medical judgment.
